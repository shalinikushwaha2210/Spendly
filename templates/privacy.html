Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect the information you provide when creating an account, such as your name and email address, as well as the expense records, categories, amounts, dates, and descriptions you choose to enter. We may also collect basic usage data such as login timestamps to help maintain the security of your account.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to provide and improve the Spendly service — storing your expenses, generating spending summaries, and enabling you to filter and review your financial history. We do not use your data for advertising or sell it to third parties.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We take reasonable technical measures to protect it from unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure. Your expense data is not shared with third parties except as strictly required to operate the service or to comply with applicable law.

4. Your Choices

You may update your account information at any time through your profile settings. You may also request deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting us. Once deleted, your data cannot be recovered.

5. Cookies and Tracking

Spendly uses session cookies to keep you logged in. We do not use third-party tracking cookies or analytics services that share your data with outside parties.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of Spendly after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

7. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the support channels listed on our website.
